About us

Bad Pug Brewing is a fictional beer company centered around the emotions of a pug. Pugs are a unique breed with a giant perinsality fit into a small package. To honor these little creatures we have created Bad Pug Brewing which features multiple creaft beers dedicated to the quirks of pugs.
